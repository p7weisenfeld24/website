Someone I Admire


Nikola Tesla




Nikola Tesla was a Serbian-American inventor of the 19th Century.Tesla's most notable
acheivment is hisdiscovery and polularization of Alternating Current(A/C).He worked
for a short time with Thomas Edison before going out on his own. He then worked with
Westinghouse to compete with Edison and his Direct Current(D/C).

I admire Tesla because he came from a normal background and was the first in his family
to study science. It was because of Tesla's innovations that or technology has advanced
to where it is today. If Edison's DC remained as the standard current, our progress
in technology would be behind where it is today.
